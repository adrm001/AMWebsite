Woodworking

A collection of some of the things I've built with wood.

In the last few years I've started to get into woodworking, and found that I really like it. I've learn most of what I know from personal experience and the internet. Luckily, there's a great wealth of online woodworking content makers, and a great community around them. I've watched videos by a great many people, but of special mention are Norm Abram of The New Yankee Workshop, The Wood Whisperer Marc Spagnuolo, and Steve Ramsey from Woodworking for Mere Mortals.

Since I've only recently started woodworking, most of my projects are utilitarian in nature, and don't tend to be very attractive. Below are a few exceptions.
