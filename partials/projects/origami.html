Origami

A collection of some of the things I've folded.

Origami is the ancient Japanese art of paper folding. Traditionally, origami models are made from a single sheet of paper, and cutting is not allowed. I started folding origami before I knew how to read, and since then it's been a hobby that I always come back to throughout the years. While I have not designed many models myself, I do have considerable experience folding complex and some super complex models.

There are many origami resources out there, but below are my favorite books:

  • Origami for the Enthusiast - John Montroll
  • Animal Origami for the Enthusiast - John Montroll
  • Origami from Angelfish to Zen - Peter Engel
  • Origami Design Secrets - Robert Lang
  • Works of Satoshi Kamiya - Satoshi Kamiya
